Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is Payload Parser free to use?
Yes! Payload Parser is completely free to use. All features are available without any cost or registration.
Q: Do you store my data?
No, we don't store any of your data. All processing happens in your browser or temporarily on our server for specific conversions, but nothing is saved to disk or database. Check our Privacy Policy for more details.
Q: How can I report a bug?
You can report bugs by selecting "Bug Report" as the subject. Please include as much detail as possible, including the input data that caused the issue (if safe to share).
Q: Can I suggest new features?
Absolutely! We love hearing feature requests from our users. Use the contact form and select "Feature Request" to share your ideas.
Q: Do you offer API access?
Currently, Payload Parser is a web-based tool only. If you're interested in API access for integration purposes, please contact us via the business email above.
Q: Can I use Payload Parser offline?
Most features work client-side in your browser and can work offline once the page is loaded. However, some advanced conversions and repairs require server processing and need an internet connection.
