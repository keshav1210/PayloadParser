We don't store your data. Ever.

What We Don't Do

Payload Parser is designed with privacy as the top priority. Here's what we explicitly DON'T do:

  • No Data Storage: We don't store any of your JSON, XML, YAML, or TOML data in databases or files
  • No Data Logging: We don't log or record your data anywhere on our servers
  • No User Accounts: We don't require login or collect personal information
  • No Tracking: We don't use cookies, analytics, or tracking scripts to monitor your activity
  • No Third-Party Sharing: We never share your data with third parties

How Our Tools Work

Most of our tools operate client-side (in your browser), but some features require server processing:

Client-Side Processing (Browser)

The majority of our tools process data entirely in your browser:

  • JSON/XML/YAML/TOML parsing and formatting
  • Basic validation and syntax highlighting
  • Simple conversions between formats

For these features, your data never leaves your device.

Server-Side Processing (Temporary)

Some advanced features require server processing:

  • Complex format conversions
  • Advanced data repair and correction
  • Large file processing

Important: When server processing is used:

  • Your data is sent to our server over a secure HTTPS connection
  • Data is processed immediately in server memory
  • Results are sent back to you
  • Your data is immediately discarded from server memory
  • Nothing is saved to disk, database, or logs
  • The entire process takes only milliseconds

Browser Storage

Our tools may use your browser's local storage for the following purposes only:

  • Saving your preference settings (theme, default format, etc.)
  • Temporarily caching the tool interface for better performance

Important: We never store your actual data (JSON, XML, etc.) in browser storage. You can clear this at any time through your browser settings.

External Links

Our blog and documentation may contain links to external websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those websites. We encourage you to read their privacy policies.

Security

We take your data security seriously:

  • HTTPS Encryption: All data transmission is encrypted using industry-standard SSL/TLS
  • No Persistence: Server processes data in memory only - never written to disk
  • Immediate Disposal: Data is discarded immediately after processing
  • No Logs: We don't log the content of your data
  • Secure Infrastructure: Our servers follow security best practices

Your data security also depends on:

  • Your device security
  • Your browser security
  • Your network connection security

Children's Privacy

Our tools are available to users of all ages. Since we don't collect any personal information, there are no special considerations for children's privacy.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change our privacy practices, we will update this page with the new information and update the "Last Updated" date at the top.
